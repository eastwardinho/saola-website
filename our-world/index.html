Materials

Made in Vietnam

Many of the world's best interior design products are created in Vietnam, but quickly exported for others to enjoy. Saola exists to change that.

We're the first interior design and lighting retailer made in Vietnam FOR Vietnam. Every piece celebrates the rich heritage of Vietnamese craftsmanship while embracing contemporary design.

From the ceramic villages of Bat Trang to the bamboo forests of the Central Highlands, our products tell the story of Vietnam's incredible artisanal traditions.
